Full-Stack LMS Platform
iNGENiO Education — Case Study
Complete learning management system for children's digital art education. From concept to launch: research-driven design, iterative development, and measurable educational impact.
View Platform Tour →

The Problem

Children's digital art education was fragmented and expensive. Parents struggled to find comprehensive, age-appropriate art programs that could scale with their child's development. Existing platforms were either too simplistic (just coloring apps) or too complex for young learners, with poor UX and high costs.

Research Phase

Conducted extensive user research with 50+ parents and 30+ art educators. Key insights: children aged 6-12 need structured progression, immediate feedback, and social learning elements. Parents valued affordability ($9.99/month) and comprehensive curriculum coverage. Educators emphasized the importance of technique fundamentals over just "fun" activities.

Design Process

Started with paper wireframes and user journey mapping. Created detailed user personas and empathy maps. Designed modular course structure with 8 progressive levels, each containing 12 lessons. Implemented gamification elements (badges, progress bars, certificates) while maintaining educational integrity.

Technical Architecture

Built on WordPress with LearnDash LMS framework. Custom PHP development for course logic, progress tracking, and automated assessments. Integrated Stripe for payments, Mailchimp for communications, and custom analytics dashboard. Responsive design optimized for tablets and mobile devices.

Iterations & Challenges

Challenge 1: Course completion rates were low (45%). Solution: Added personalized learning paths and email reminders, increasing completion to 78%.

Challenge 2: Mobile experience was poor. Solution: Complete responsive redesign with touch-optimized controls and offline lesson downloads.

Challenge 3: Parent engagement was minimal. Solution: Added parent dashboard with progress reports and monthly newsletters, boosting retention by 40%.

Measurable Impact

  • 1,200+ active students across 15 countries
  • 85% course completion rate (industry average: 15%)
  • $120K annual recurring revenue at $9.99/month
  • 4.8/5 parent satisfaction rating
  • 60% student improvement in digital art skills (pre/post assessments)

Key Technologies

WordPress • LearnDash LMS • PHP • MySQL • Stripe API • Mailchimp API • Custom JavaScript • Responsive CSS Grid • SVG animations

About

I've had the kind of career that gives you range — and I've leaned into it. I started my working life as an analyst in the Investment Research division at Goldman Sachs in London — a serious apprenticeship in research, analysis, and producing careful, high-stakes work for institutional clients. I came home to Cape Town and co-founded Fineday Design Studio, where I spent seven years building food brands and packaging from scratch — work that went on to include two licensed international pattern collaborations (Studio E Fabrics and Nested Bean, both in the US).

After that I shifted closer to the technical side as a Junior Data Analyst, then back to building something of my own. Today I run iNGENiO Education — a full-stack digital art learning platform for children — where I own the platform architecture, the LMS, the UX, and the brand. Every pixel and every line of code.

What all of that adds up to is a genuinely unusual combination: design instinct, e-commerce fluency, platform & LMS development, and a growing practice in AI-enabled tools. It's the kind of toolkit that suits small and mid-sized businesses that don't need an agency — they need a trusted pair of hands.
